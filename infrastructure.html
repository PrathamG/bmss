 Infrastructure 

We have a full fledged steel manufacturing facility with a Steel Melting Shop, Open Die Forge Shop, and Machining facilities. We make every effort to keep updating our infrastructure in order to give our clients the best experience. This year, we set up a full-line, 10000 sq.ft service center with Bandsaw Machines, a Lathe Machine, a 5 ton Weigh Scale, a Horizontal Boring Machine, and a 10 ton Overhead Crane. We also have multiple warehouses in Kalamboli where we keep ready stock. These facilities allow us to fulfill customer demands faster, thereby reducing their downtime and costs.
